Case Study

### Advanced Chatbots & Conversational AI

Conversational AIAgentic AINLP

#### Client

A global enterprise needed an intelligent virtual assistant that could handle large volumes of customer support interactions, mimic human-like dialogue flow, verify customer accounts, provide targeted FAQs, seamlessly access internal databases, and scale across regions and languages.

#### Approach

We developed an agentic AI system with advanced NLP capabilities that follows human-like conversation strategies and retains context. It can securely access customer data for account verification and fetch personalized information (e.g., subscription status, recent purchases). The service is containerized and orchestrated with Kubernetes, ensuring smooth scalability under unpredictable workloads. The chatbot now handles more than 70% of common queries 24/7, speeds up resolution times by verifying accounts instantly, and reduces operational costs, decreasing wait times while freeing human agents to focus on complex interactions.

### Real-Time Support & Sales Conversation Agent

Speech RecognitionAgentic AI

#### Client

A sales and customer support team in a rapidly growing company needed an intelligent assistant during their calls to enhance sales team efficiency, provide real-time customer data, and deliver on-the-fly troubleshooting or upselling suggestions without disrupting the conversation flow.

#### Approach

Our team built a real-time AI agent assistant that uses speech-to-text technology to transcribe calls instantly and analyze context. The system accesses customer data (e.g., account history, past tickets, product interests) in the background, surfacing relevant knowledge base articles and product recommendations. It also delivers best-practice conversation tips, upsell prompts, and troubleshooting scripts, enabling agents to improve customer satisfaction and close more deals. As a result, average handle time and first-call resolution rates have improved significantly, and sales conversion rates have increased thanks to personalized real-time suggestions.

### Invoice Classification at 98.5% Accuracy

Deep Learning

#### Client

A major US enterprise needed a faster, more reliable way to classify thousands of uniquely formatted invoices daily, eliminating manual errors and providing real-time financial reporting insights.

#### Approach

We developed a custom AI classification system capable of recognizing invoice structures from multiple vendors. The model achieves 98.5% precision and uses confidence predictions to flag low-confidence cases for human review. An integrated analytics dashboard provides real-time monitoring, comprehensive reporting, and flexible configuration, allowing finance teams to add new invoice types or adjust thresholds without coding. With automated processing, the enterprise cut error rates, reduced manual overhead, and scaled to 10x more clients without increasing headcount, freeing the finance department to focus on strategic tasks.

### Parkinson's Diagnosis Via Smartphone

Computer Vision

#### Client

A VC-backed neuroscience startup sought a non-invasive, cost-effective smartphone-based method to detect early-stage Parkinson's symptoms in regions with limited specialist access where traditional screening tools were expensive and scarce.

#### Approach

Our team built a computer vision algorithm that analyzes pupillary response using a standard smartphone camera. By identifying subtle movement patterns and reaction times of the pupil under varying lighting, the model pinpoints early indicators of Parkinson's. We also developed a lightweight mobile app that guides users through the test, ensuring consistency in data capture. This smartphone-based solution expands access to early detection, enabling healthcare providers to triage at-risk patients quickly and bridging critical gaps in neurology care.

### Speech Therapy Diagnosis

Speech Recognition

#### Client

A healthcare technology partner aimed to streamline and automate initial screening for speech pathology patients facing lengthy wait times for specialized therapists.

#### Approach

We created a speech analysis engine using natural language processing and acoustic feature extraction to detect phonetic, prosodic, and linguistic irregularities in spoken inputs. The system evaluates pronunciation accuracy, fluency, and rate of speech in real time, then generates personalized exercises and progress metrics via an intuitive dashboard. Therapists can review results, provide feedback, and adjust exercise difficulty remotely. This platform reduces therapist workload, expands therapy accessibility, and delivers consistent progress tracking, leading to faster patient onboarding, improved therapy outcomes, and more personalized, adaptive care.
