### How Do We Know If It’s Time to Hire a Fractional Head of AI or Chief Artificial Intelligence Officer?

##### If you can’t confidently answer “yes” to these questions, it’s probably time to bring in a Head of AI/CAIO:

Do we know where AI can create the most value in our business? Yes/No

Do we have a clear and actionable AI strategy that aligns with our business goals? Yes/No

Are we in a position to 1) plan, 2) manage, and 3) measure the results of an AI implementation strategy? Yes/No

Are we equipped to identify and implement the best AI tools and technologies for our needs? Yes/No

Can we integrate AI into our operations without overwhelming our already busy team? Yes/No

Do we know how to measure the potential ROI of AI—like cost savings, increased revenue, or better productivity? Yes/No

Can we effectively address concerns from team members about how AI might affect their roles? Yes/No

Are we confident that AI will enhance, not replace, existing jobs in our organization? Yes/No

Do we have a plan to stay ahead of our competitors in adopting AI innovations? Yes/No

##### If these questions reveal gaps in your organization’s readiness for AI, a fractional Head of AI/CAIO can provide the expertise, leadership, and strategic direction needed to move from ‘thinking about AI’ to ‘actually executing  a strategy’. ### FAQs

#### [What is a Head of AI?](https://headofai.ai/\#1)

A Head of AI is a senior executive responsible for developing and implementing an organization’s artificial intelligence strategy. They oversee AI initiatives, manage AI teams, and ensure AI technologies align with business objectives to drive innovation and competitive advantage.

#### [If we already have a CTO or Head of IT, how will our Head of AI interact with them?](https://headofai.ai/\#1739280774496-e666d2ae-fd2c)

Our Head of AI will collaborate closely with your CTO or Head of IT, ensuring seamless integration of AI strategies with your existing technology infrastructure. They will focus on leveraging AI to enhance your current systems while ensuring alignment with your broader IT and business goals. Learn more about why you need both [CTO and Head of AI](https://headofai.ai/cto-vs-caio-why-you-need-both-to-stay-ahead-in-the-tech-game/) for better implementation of AI strategies.

#### [What are your contractual terms?](https://headofai.ai/\#2)

Our services are offered on a monthly contract basis, providing flexibility and allowing you to scale AI leadership according to your needs.

#### [What is included in the monthly fee?](https://headofai.ai/\#3)

The monthly fee includes a minimum number of half days per month of Head of AI time, project leadership, AI workshops and ideation sessions, board-level advisory support, and email support. Additionally, you will receive a quarterly AI Game Plan Report and delivery of a One Page AI Game Plan.

#### [How do I know if it’s time to hire a Head of AI?](https://headofai.ai/\#5)

If you’re struggling with inefficient data usage, keeping up with competitors, or lacking internal AI expertise, it might be time to consider a Head of AI. They can help develop and implement a comprehensive AI strategy, drive data-driven decision-making, and ensure your business stays ahead of the curve with timely AI initiatives and efficient AI systems.

#### [How quickly can we start seeing results?](https://headofai.ai/\#6)

Typically, you’ll start seeing results within the first 90 days. Our process is designed to deliver high-impact projects with measurable ROI, focusing on the most valuable and least disruptive initiatives first.

#### [How will AI impact my team’s workload?](https://headofai.ai/\#7)

Our goal is to implement AI solutions that reduce manual and repetitive tasks, freeing up your team to focus on more strategic activities. We also provide AI training to upskill your staff, helping them embrace and effectively use new AI tools.

#### [What industries do you specialize in?](https://headofai.ai/\#8)

Our Heads of AI have experience across various industries, and our Outcome-Driven AI Framework ensures that we can tailor solutions to meet the unique needs of your business, regardless of industry.

#### [Can AI help with ethical decision-making in our business?](https://headofai.ai/\#9)

Yes, our Heads of AI are trained in ethical AI practices and will help you establish guidelines and practices that ensure responsible AI use in your business.

#### [What kind of AI projects will we work on?](https://headofai.ai/\#10)

Based on your One Page AI Game Plan, we will typically work with you to select 1-3 key projects initially with the highest potential ROI, the lowest risk, and the least impact on your team’s time. These projects could range from automating routine processes to implementing advanced data analytics or customer engagement tools. Our aim is to demonstrate how AI projects, when delivered to a high standard, will deliver significant ROI as well as create a blueprint for all future projects.
