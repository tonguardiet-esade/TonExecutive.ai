## We accelerate Data and AI adoption to positively impact people and organizations.

![Strategy & Transformation](https://www.artefact.com//wp-content/uploads/2024/09/strategy-transformation-icone.png)

Strategy

& Transformation

We accompany companies from strategy to operations and adoption. We leverage all types of data across organizations to accelerate their transformation and create value. Our hackathons, GenAI Academy and School of Data help companies educate and train their teams.

![AI Acceleration](https://www.artefact.com//wp-content/uploads/2024/09/ai-acceleration.webp)

AI

Generative & Agents

Grounded on solid data foundations, we develop AI solutions using the latest AI technologies, Generative AI and Agentic AI (AI agents), adapted to industry sectors and tailor-made per departments (Sales & Marketing, Customer Support, Supply Chain, Manufacturing, Finance, HR,…). We have dedicated teams with thorough expertise, working together beyond frontiers.

![Data Foundations & BI](https://www.artefact.com//wp-content/uploads/2024/09/data-ai-days-4-1.webp)

Data

Foundations & BI

We create a foundation for effective data governance and management through a framework that drives value and compliance. Our data analytics and BI tools help enterprises work smarter and drive better results. We also combine data and environmental transformations for greater impact.

![IT & Data Platforms](https://www.artefact.com//wp-content/uploads/2024/09/it-data-platforms.webp)

IT

& Data Platforms

Data-Centric IT focuses on leveraging data as a strategic asset, while Cloud Services provide scalable, secure infrastructure. Together, they enable smarter decision-making, agility, and optimized IT operations. Artefact is tech agnostic and we team up with industry biggest Cloud Providers.

![Marketing Data-driven](https://www.artefact.com//wp-content/uploads/2024/09/marketing-data-driven.webp)

Marketing

Data & Digital

Working together between marketing specialists with data scientists and data analysts, we manage campaigns and marketing objectives using advanced cross-channel mechanisms. Through Customer Data Platforms, we empower marketing organizations and increase their marketing ROI.
