# Fractional CTO & Advisory

Partnering with leaders to de-risk complex technology investments and transform your AI and cloud strategy into a durable competitive advantage.

## Who I Work With

I provide expert counsel to leaders who are navigating high-stakes technology decisions and transformations. My clients typically include:

- 1



### C-Suite Leaders (CEOs, CTOs, CIOs)



Seeking to align their technology roadmap with business objectives, validate a strategic direction, or prepare their organization for the next wave of innovation.

- 2



### Boards of Directors



Needing expert, independent guidance on technology trends, cybersecurity risks, and oversight of large-scale digital transformation or AI initiatives.

- 3



### Private Equity & Venture Capital Firms



Requiring technical due diligence, portfolio company audits, or strategic advice to assess and maximize the value of technology-driven investments.


## Advisory Offerings

My services are structured as clear, high-impact engagements designed to deliver tangible outcomes.

[Inquire About This Service](https://rajatpandit.com/services)

- ### Strategic AI Roadmap



A comprehensive engagement to assess your organization's AI readiness, identify high-impact opportunities, and develop a multi-year, actionable roadmap for successful adoption and scaling

- ### Technical Due Diligence



A rigorous and unbiased assessment of a company's technology stack, architecture, team, and processes. Ideal for investors pre-acquisition or for boards seeking to validate their current technology strategy.

- ### Executive & Board Briefings



Concise, high-impact sessions designed to demystify complex topics like Generative AI, MLOps, or multi-cloud economics, equipping leadership with the critical knowledge to make informed strategic decisions.

- ### Retained Advisory



An ongoing partnership providing consistent access to strategic counsel. This service is ideal for organizations that need continuous guidance through a major transformation, product launch, or market expansion.
